STARTING GUIDES FOR TRAINERS

Since you're still starting, we advise you to look for the closest professor you can find. Don't worry, they're always glad to see another aspiring trainer enter into the world!

Professor Birch running as fast as he can as he knowing there's another potential talent!
The one that's immediately available! Although there are many types of Poké Balls, as a beginner you might not have the funds yet to use more specialized Poké Balls so we advise you to whittle down that wild Pokémon's health point to the lowest you can and use your good ol' Poké Ball.
There are many ways to make you and your Pokémon team stronger such as fighting wild Pokémon, leaving them in the daycare center, and ultimately by challenging others to a Poké Battle!
As a professor once said, there's a time and place for everything so don't force others to a battle and do it in moderation!
